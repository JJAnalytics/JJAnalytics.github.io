Intro

Meet Jaron – Data Analyst | Bridging Engineering Precision & Strategic Insight

With a 13-year tenure in the Royal Navy and Royal Marines, I honed expertise in high-stakes environments, optimising operational workflows, managing mission-critical record-keeping systems, and leading inventory controls with meticulous attention to detail. My roles—from aircraft handling to vehicle maintenance—demanded rigorous precision and a systems-thinking mindset, traits I now channel into transforming raw data into actionable intelligence.

Transitioning to tech, I spent 5 years as a Computer Technician at OTEC Computer Services, resolving complex technical challenges and building trusted client partnerships. This dual foundation in defence operations and IT problem-solving fuels my unique perspective in data analytics: merging analytical rigor with real-world pragmatism.

Today, I leverage tools like Excel, Tableau, Python, SQL, and Power BI to clean, analyse, and visualize data, turning ambiguity into actionable strategies. My expertise lies in:

• Identifying inefficiencies in workflows and data pipelines.

• Building dashboards that empower teams to make informed decisions.

• Translating technical insights into clear, business-focused recommendations.

Whether forecasting trends, diagnosing operational bottlenecks, or automating reporting, I thrive on helping organisations unlock the full potential of their data. Let’s connect to discuss how data can drive your next strategic move.

Projects

Production-Inventory-Insights

A data-driven solution that provides actionable insights for optimising production processes and inventory management. This project uses advanced analytics to help businesses improve operational efficiency, reduce costs, and make smarter, data-backed decisions.

Evaluating Health Expo Impact

This project evaluates the impact of health expos on community awareness, engagement, and behaviour change through data analysis and evaluation methods.

Longevity & Population Density Global Insights

Longevity & Population Density Global Insights is a data analysis project exploring the relationship between population density and life expectancy across countries. It uncovers insights into how factors like healthcare, economy, and social systems impact longevity, revealing that population density alone doesn't determine life expectancy.
